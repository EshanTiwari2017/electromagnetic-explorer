The Spectrum

The spectrum includes all types of electromagnetic radiation. These travel through space, ranging from huge radio waves to tiny gamma rays.

Frequency & Energy

As wavelength gets shorter, frequency and energy increase. This is why X-rays can pass through objects while visible light cannot.

Visible Portion
