Electromagnetic Wave Details

The Spectrum

The spectrum includes all types of electromagnetic radiation. These are energy waves that travel through space, ranging from huge radio waves to tiny gamma rays.

Frequency & Energy

As the wavelength gets shorter, the frequency and energy increase. This is why Gamma rays and X-rays can pass through objects while visible light cannot.

Visible Portion

Visible light is just a tiny sliver in the middle. Human eyes are only tuned to see waves between 400 and 750 nanometers. Everything else is invisible to us!
